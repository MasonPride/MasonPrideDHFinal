2016

The 2016 Mr. Olympia Men's Classic Physique competition marked the first year of the Classic Physique division, taking place in Las Vegas, NV on September 16th-17th 2016. Danny Hester made history by becoming the first ever winner of this division.

Results:

  1. Danny Hester - USA
  2. Arash Rahbar - USA
  3. Sadik Hadzovic - USA
  4. Breon Ansley - USA
  5. Darrem Charles - Trinidad
  6. Robert Timms - USA
  7. Ko Chandetka - USA
  8. Shavis Higa- USA
  9. Terrence Ruffin - USA
  10. Stan McQuay - USA
