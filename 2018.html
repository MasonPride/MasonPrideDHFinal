2018

The 2018 Mr. Olympia Men's Classic Physique competition was the third year of the Classic Physique division, taking place in Las Vegas, NV on September 13th 2018.

  2. Chris Bumstead - Canada

  7. Steve Laureus - USA

  9. Terrence Ruffin - USA
